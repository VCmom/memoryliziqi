Play the Li Ziqi Chinese Memory Test

Enhance your memory by recalling Chinese words inspired by Li Ziqi's videos.

Game Rules

The Li Ziqi Chinese Memory Test is designed to challenge your memory and help you learn Chinese vocabulary. The game consists of 20 levels, each lasting 5 seconds. In each level, you'll be shown a question about Chinese culture, foods, or items featured in Li Ziqi's videos. You have 5 seconds to read and memorize the question.

After the countdown, the question remains visible for 10 more seconds while you type your answer. If you recall it correctly, you'll earn points based on the level. The difficulty increases with each level, making it a fun and engaging way to enhance your memory and language skills.

Scoring System

Your performance in the Li Ziqi Chinese Memory Test is evaluated based on the following scoring system:

  • Level 1: 5 points
  • Level 2: 10 points
  • Level 3: 15 points
  • ...
  • Level 20: 100 points

The total possible score is 1050 points. Achieving a score of 840 points or above is considered excellent. The higher your score, the better your memory and language proficiency!

About Li Ziqi

Li Ziqi is a Chinese video blogger, entrepreneur, and internet celebrity known for her videos showcasing traditional Chinese culture and rural life. Her content beautifully captures the essence of Chinese traditions, cuisine, and craftsmanship.

By playing the Li Ziqi Chinese Memory Test, you not only challenge your memory but also immerse yourself in the rich vocabulary inspired by Li Ziqi's enchanting videos.

Absolutely! The game is optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.
